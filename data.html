Overview

Turn data into decisions, built on modern platforms, analytics, and AI/ML embedded in operations.

Data is the raw material of every smarter outcome. Smarter Data builds the modern platforms, analytics and machine learning that turn raw data into decisions, and keeps it trusted, governed and ready for AI at enterprise scale.

What we do

How it works

This is the foundation Semantic Engineering depends on: the knowledge graph that grounds every copilot and agent is only as trustworthy as the governed data beneath it. Smarter Data makes that foundation real.

Semantic Engineering at the core. The Semantic Engineering knowledge graph is built on trusted data: lakehouse, lineage and governance are what let Breeze.AI and ASIMOV ground their outputs in enterprise truth. Get data right and every other area gets smarter.
