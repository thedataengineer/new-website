Overview

Re-wire how work flows: intelligent automation that removes friction end to end.

Most enterprises run on processes designed for a pre-AI world. Smarter Processes re-wires how work flows, combining automation, integration and intelligence to remove friction end to end, not just digitize the steps that were already there.

What we do

How it works

We start from evidence: process mining shows where work really breaks down, then we engineer the friction out with RPA, intelligent document processing and SPEX-powered agentic automation that acts across systems, governed by Semantic Engineering.

Semantic Engineering at the core. Process knowledge, steps, rules, exceptions and ownership, lives in the Semantic Engineering graph, so SPEX agents automate against the real process under validation gates, not a stale diagram.
