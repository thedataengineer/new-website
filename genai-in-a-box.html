GenAI-in-a-box

Run modern AI on-premise or fully air-gapped when your data cannot leave the building, at a substantially lower five-year cost of ownership.

On-premises GenAI applianceAir-gap deployableGoverned inside the perimeter
The problem

Some workloads cannot leave the building. AI spend rises anyway.

Regulation, contracts, and risk posture keep certain data inside your perimeter. Cloud GenAI asks that data to travel, and for these workloads the answer is no. The usual result is a standstill: the highest-stakes use cases are the ones that never ship.

The second problem compounds the first. GenAI spend grows with every prompt and every new use case while the value it returns goes unmeasured. GenAI-in-a-box resolves both at once: governed AI where the data already lives, with economics you can defend over five years.

The appliance

Governed GenAI inside your perimeter

GenAI-in-a-box is our on-premises GenAI appliance. It runs governed AI inside the perimeter for the data that must stay there.

Runs where your data lives

Deploy on-premise or fully air-gapped. Prompts, outputs, and the data behind them stay inside the building instead of transiting a public API.

Governed from day one

Governance is part of the appliance, not a later project. Usage runs under controls you set and can inspect, inside your own perimeter.

Current capability on owned infrastructure

A data constraint does not have to become an AI constraint. The appliance brings modern GenAI capability to the workloads cloud services cannot touch.

A cost model you own

Owned capacity replaces metered consumption. That single change is what drives the five-year economics.

How it works

AI FinOps meets on-prem economics

The economics rest on two disciplines: measure what AI returns, and own the capacity that produces it.

Spend tied to measured value

We run AI FinOps on every deployment: usage metered, cost attributed, value tracked against both. The gap between what AI costs and what it returns stops being invisible.

A flatter cost curve

Consumption pricing grows with every new use case. Owned capacity does not. As adoption spreads across the enterprise, the unit economics improve instead of eroding.

A five-year number with a basis

We model total cost of ownership over five years against your workloads and constraints, and we state the TCO claim together with the engagement it was modeled on.

Built into Smarter Platform

GenAI-in-a-box powers our Smarter Platform pillar, TokenOps included. When AI infrastructure cost is the wider problem, that is the wider conversation.
